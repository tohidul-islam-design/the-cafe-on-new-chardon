Our Story

Small town feel in the big city. The West End's favorite breakfast & lunch spot.

A West End Classic

In a city of constantly changing trends and fleeting pop-up shops, The Cafe on New Chardon remains a steadfast classic. We consider ourselves a survivor—bringing you that nostalgic, unpretentious small-town deli experience right in the heart of Boston's bustling West End.

We aren't trying to reinvent the wheel; we're just making sure it tastes delicious. Home of the legendary Steak Bomb and the best breakfast deals in town, we serve honest food at honest prices.

Why Locals Love Us

  • Affordable prices in downtown Boston
  • Friendly staff who know your name
